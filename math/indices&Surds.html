indices & surds

index laws

$$a^m \times a^n = a^{m+n}$$

$$\frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}$$

$$(a^m)^n = a^{m \times n}$$

$$a^0 = 1$$

$$a^{-m} = \frac{1}{a^m}$$

$$(a \times b)^n = a^n \times b^n$$

$$(\frac{a}{b})^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n}$$

$$x^{\frac{1}{4}} \times x^{\frac{1}{4}} = x^{\frac{1}{2}}$$

$$\sqrt[-1]{x} = \text{unidentified}$$

$$x^{\frac{y}{z}} = \sqrt[z]{x^y} = (x^y)^{\frac{1}{z}}$$
